Note to readers

The Consumer Price Index (CPI) measures the rate of price change experienced by Canadian consumers. It is calculated by comparing the cost of a fixed basket of goods and services purchased by consumers over time. The CPI is not seasonally adjusted. For more information, consult Statistics Canada's Consumer Price Index portal.
